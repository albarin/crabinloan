I thought my delivery was wrong when I opened the box and saw only the printer, I though they forgot to include the AMS, but it turns out it comes inside the printer itself ðŸ¤­
I was also surprised to see it came with three PLA filament spools, I expected none, so that was a nice bonus even though they were only 250g each (green, orange and white).

I was hoping to use a spare Besta module I have from my old home office to put the printer on, but I wasn't sure whether it would fit or the vibration would be a problem. It turns out it fits perfectly and for now the vibration is not an issue at all, so I'm happy with that minimal setup for to start with.

<Image width="300" height="200" src="/images/blog/bambu-lab-p1s-on-besta.jpeg" alt="Bambu Lab P1S on Besta module" />

The first thing I printed is, of course, the famous benchy. The model came preloaded in the printer's SD card, so I just had to select it from the screen and start printing. I choose orange PLA for it, and the result was nice!
