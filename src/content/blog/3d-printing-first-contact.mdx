I'm a member of the [BCN Engineering](https://bcneng.org/) community Slack and there's a #3d-printing channel, so I asked there for help and recommendations for getting started in the 3D printing world. I got some advice and links to resources.

They recommended to create profiles in the following websites and start exploring 3D models to see what I would like to print and learn about modelling:

- [Printables](https://www.printables.com)
- [Cults 3D](https://cults3d.com)
- [MakerWorld](https://makerworld.com)
- [Tinkercad](https://www.tinkercad.com)
